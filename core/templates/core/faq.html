Frequently Asked Questions

// Everything you need to know about investing with Qubix

How do I start investing with Qubix?

Getting started with Qubix is simple:

  1. Create an account – Sign up with your email and complete verification
  2. Deposit funds – Add cash to your account using cryptocurrency
  3. Browse investments – Explore Stocks, Minerals, Real Estate, or Physical Assets
  4. Start investing – Choose an asset and amount to invest

You can start with as little as $100 for real estate or $10 for stocks.

What documents do I need to verify my account?

To verify your Qubix account, you'll need:

  • A valid government-issued ID (Passport, Driver's License, or National ID)
  • Proof of address (Utility bill, bank statement, or government letter dated within 3 months)
  • A selfie for identity confirmation

Verification typically takes 1-2 business days.

What investment options are available on Qubix?

Qubix offers a diverse range of investment opportunities:

  • Stocks – Invest in shares of major public companies
  • Minerals – Gold, Silver, Platinum, and other precious metals
  • Real Estate – Fractional ownership in income-generating properties
  • Physical Assets – Vault-stored bullion with optional delivery

Each asset class has different risk profiles and return potentials.

How are real estate dividends calculated?

Real estate dividends are calculated based on:

  • Rental income collected from tenants
  • Occupancy rates of the property
  • Operating expenses (maintenance, management fees, insurance)
  • Reserve funds set aside for future capital improvements

Dividends are typically paid monthly or quarterly, depending on the property.

What fees does Qubix charge?

Qubix maintains transparent, competitive fees:

  • Trading Fee: 0.5% per transaction (buy/sell)
  • Real Estate Management: 1.5% of gross rental income
  • Vault Storage: No fee for first year, then 0.5% annually
  • Withdrawal Fee: 5% of withdrawal amount (covers processing)
  • Deposit Fee: No fee for crypto deposits

No hidden fees. All charges are clearly displayed before you confirm any transaction.

How do I change my password?

To change your password:

  1. Click on your avatar in the top right corner
  2. Select "Profile" from the dropdown
  3. Scroll to the "Security" section
  4. Click "Change Password" and follow the instructions

For security reasons, you'll need to enter your current password before creating a new one.

How do I update my profile information?

You can update your profile information at any time:

  1. Click on your avatar in the navigation bar
  2. Go to "Profile" page
  3. Edit your name, phone number, country, or wallet address
  4. Click "Save Changes"

Note: Email address and username cannot be changed. Contact support if you need assistance.

How do I withdraw my funds?

To withdraw funds from your Qubix account:

  1. Go to the "Withdraw" page from your dashboard
  2. Select your preferred cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH, USDT, or LTC)
  3. Enter your wallet address and withdrawal amount
  4. Pay the 5% processing fee
  5. Confirm the withdrawal

Withdrawals are typically processed within 24-48 hours after fee payment confirmation.

How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawal processing times:

  • Fee payment confirmation: Immediate after blockchain confirmation
  • Admin review: 1-2 business days
  • Crypto transfer: 1-3 business days depending on network
  • Total estimated time: 2-5 business days

You can track your withdrawal status in the Transactions section of your dashboard.

Is my money safe with Qubix?

Security is our top priority at Qubix:

  • SSL Encryption – All data is encrypted in transit
  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) – Optional extra security layer
  • Cold Storage – Majority of crypto assets stored offline
  • Vault Security – Physical assets stored in insured, guarded vaults
  • Regular Audits – Independent security audits performed quarterly

We never have direct access to your funds. All transactions require your explicit confirmation.

How do I enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)?

To enable 2FA on your account:

  1. Go to Profile → Security Settings
  2. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
  3. Scan the QR code with Google Authenticator or Authy
  4. Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app
  5. Save your backup codes in a secure location

2FA adds an extra layer of security to your account, requiring a code from your phone to log in.
