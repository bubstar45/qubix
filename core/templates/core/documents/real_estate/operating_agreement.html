Article 1
Formation, Purpose & Property

This Operating Agreement ("Agreement") governs the fractional ownership and co-investment arrangement in the real property known as {{ property_name }}, located at {{ property_location }} ("the Property"). Qubix Real Estate Ltd. ("the Manager") serves as the managing member responsible for all operational, financial, and administrative functions related to the Property.

The purpose of this arrangement is to facilitate fractional real estate investment, providing Members with proportional economic interests including rental income distributions and capital appreciation upon the eventual sale of the Property.

Article 2
Management Rights & Responsibilities
The Manager has sole authority over property management decisions including tenant selection, lease terms, maintenance, and capital improvements. Investor Members are passive investors with no day-to-day management rights.
The Manager shall maintain proper books of account and provide quarterly financial reports to all Members. Annual audited financial statements shall be provided within 90 days of each fiscal year end.
The Manager may engage third-party property managers, accountants, legal counsel, and other service providers as deemed necessary, the costs of which are property operating expenses.

Net rental income shall be distributed to Members on a {{ distribution_frequency }} basis, in proportion to each Member's ownership percentage. Distributions are calculated as follows:

Article 4
Voting Rights
Major Decisions — Sale of the Property, refinancing, or capital calls exceeding {{ major_decision_threshold }} require approval of Members holding >50% of shares.
Routine Operations — All day-to-day management decisions are at the sole discretion of the Manager and do not require Member approval.
Each share carries one vote. Votes may be cast electronically via the Qubix investor portal within 14 days of any notice of major decision.
Article 5
Transfer Restrictions & Exit Provisions
Lock-up Period: Shares may not be transferred or sold during the initial lock-up period of {{ lockup_period }} from the date of acquisition.
Secondary Market: After the lock-up period, Members may list shares for sale on the Qubix secondary marketplace, subject to platform terms and a transfer fee of {{ transfer_fee }}.
Property Sale: Upon sale of the Property, net proceeds shall be distributed to Members in proportion to ownership percentages after repayment of any debt and payment of sale costs.
Article 6
Fees Disclosure
Platform Fee: A one-time platform fee is charged on initial share purchase (disclosed at point of sale).
Management Fee: {{ management_fee_rate }} of gross rental income, charged monthly.
Performance Fee: {{ performance_fee }} of capital gain upon sale of the Property, above a hurdle rate of {{ hurdle_rate }}.
Transfer Fee: {{ transfer_fee }} per secondary market transaction.
