How Qubix Works

A complete step-by-step guide to getting crypto, depositing funds, investing in assets, buying physical gold, and withdrawing your money.

How to Get Cryptocurrency

Qubix accepts deposits in cryptocurrency only. If you do not already own crypto, here is how to acquire it before depositing into your Qubix account.

1
Choose a Cryptocurrency Exchange
Sign up on a reputable cryptocurrency exchange in your country. Popular options include Coinbase, Binance, Kraken, and Bybit. The exchange will allow you to purchase crypto using your bank account, debit card, or credit card.
2
Complete Identity Verification on the Exchange
Most exchanges require you to verify your identity (KYC) before you can buy cryptocurrency. This typically involves uploading a government-issued ID and taking a selfie. The process takes 5–30 minutes depending on the exchange.
3
Purchase Your Cryptocurrency
Once verified, use your bank account, debit card, or credit card to purchase the cryptocurrency of your choice. We recommend buying the exact amount you intend to deposit into Qubix, plus a small buffer to cover network transaction fees (typically $1–$5 worth of extra crypto).
Tip: Use USDT for simplicity If you are new to crypto, USDT (Tether) is the easiest option. Its value stays equal to $1.00 USD, so there is no risk of the price changing between when you buy it and when you deposit it into Qubix. You will always know exactly how much you are depositing.
4
You Are Ready to Deposit
Once you have cryptocurrency in your exchange wallet, you are ready to deposit it into your Qubix account. Follow the Deposit guide in the next tab to complete the process.

How to Deposit Funds

Deposits are made exclusively through cryptocurrency. Once your deposit is confirmed by the blockchain, funds are reflected in your available cash balance and can be used immediately to invest.

1
Log In and Click "Deposit" on Your Dashboard
Once logged into your Qubix account, click the Deposit button on your main dashboard. You will be taken to the deposit flow immediately.
2
Select Your Cryptocurrency and Enter the Amount
Choose which cryptocurrency you will be sending (e.g. Bitcoin, USDT, Ethereum) and enter the amount you wish to deposit in USD. The system will automatically calculate the equivalent amount in your chosen cryptocurrency at the current market rate.
3
You Will Receive a Unique Wallet Address and Exact Amount
The system generates a unique deposit wallet address for your transaction. You will see the exact amount to send and the exact address to send it to.
