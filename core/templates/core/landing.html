How Qubix Works

A complete step-by-step guide to getting crypto, depositing funds, investing in assets, buying physical gold, and withdrawing your money.

How to Get Cryptocurrency

Qubix accepts deposits in cryptocurrency only. If you do not already own crypto, here is how to acquire it before depositing into your Qubix account.

1
Choose a Cryptocurrency Exchange
Sign up on a reputable cryptocurrency exchange in your country. Popular options include Coinbase, Binance, Kraken, and Bybit. The exchange will allow you to purchase crypto using your bank account, debit card, or credit card.
🟠
Bitcoin (BTC)
The most widely accepted and liquid cryptocurrency. Accepted on all major exchanges worldwide.
🔵
Ethereum (ETH)
Second largest by market cap. Fast and widely available on all exchanges.
🟢
USDT (Tether)
A stablecoin pegged to the US Dollar. No price volatility. Recommended for first-time depositors.
2
Complete Identity Verification on the Exchange
Most exchanges require you to verify your identity (KYC) before you can buy cryptocurrency. This typically involves uploading a government-issued ID and taking a selfie. The process takes 5–30 minutes depending on the exchange.
3
Purchase Your Cryptocurrency
Once verified, use your bank account, debit card, or credit card to purchase the cryptocurrency of your choice. We recommend buying the exact amount you intend to deposit into Qubix, plus a small buffer to cover network transaction fees (typically $1–$5 worth of extra crypto).
Tip: Use USDT for simplicity If you are new to crypto, USDT (Tether) is the easiest option. Its value stays equal to $1.00 USD, so there is no risk of the price changing between when you buy it and when you deposit it into Qubix. You will always know exactly how much you are depositing.
4
You Are Ready to Deposit
Once you have cryptocurrency in your exchange wallet, you are ready to deposit it into your Qubix account. Follow the Deposit guide in the next tab to complete the process.

How to Deposit Funds

Deposits are made exclusively through cryptocurrency. Once your deposit is confirmed by the blockchain, funds are reflected in your available cash balance and can be used immediately to invest.

1
Log In and Click "Deposit" on Your Dashboard
Once logged into your Qubix account, click the Deposit button on your main dashboard. You will be taken to the deposit flow immediately.
2
Select Your Cryptocurrency and Enter the Amount
Choose which cryptocurrency you will be sending (e.g. Bitcoin, USDT, Ethereum) and enter the amount you wish to deposit in USD. The system will automatically calculate the equivalent amount in your chosen cryptocurrency at the current market rate.
3
You Will Receive a Unique Wallet Address and Exact Amount
The system generates a unique deposit wallet address for your transaction. You will see the exact amount to send and the exact address to send it to.

Send the Payment from Your External Wallet or Exchange
Open your cryptocurrency exchange or wallet app (Coinbase, Binance, Trust Wallet, etc.), paste the address, enter the exact amount, and confirm the transaction. Do not close the Qubix page until you have successfully sent the payment.
5
Click "I Have Sent the Payment" to Notify Our System
Once you have completed the transfer from your external wallet, return to Qubix and click the confirmation button. This notifies our verification system to begin monitoring the blockchain for your transaction.
6
Wait for Verification — 30 Minutes to 1 Hour
Our system monitors the blockchain and verifies your transaction automatically. This process takes between 30 minutes and 1 hour depending on network congestion. You do not need to stay on the page — you will receive an email notification when your deposit is confirmed.

How to Invest in Assets

Once your deposit is confirmed and your cash balance is funded, you can invest in stocks, real estate, precious metals, and commodities. Here is how the investment process works.

1
Go to the Markets or Investments Section
Navigate to the Markets tab in your dashboard. You can browse all available assets by category: Stocks, Precious Metals, Real Estate, and Commodities. Use the search bar to find a specific asset by name or ticker symbol.
2
Select an Asset and Review the Details
Click on any asset to view its live price, historical performance chart, asset description, risk rating, and any dividend or payout information. Take time to review this information before investing.
3
Enter Your Investment Amount and Confirm
Enter the amount you want to invest in USD. The system will show you how many units or shares you will receive at the current price. Review the order summary and click Confirm Investment to execute.
Fractional Investing Available You do not need to buy a whole share. All assets on Qubix support fractional investing. You can invest any amount from $10 upward, and the system will allocate the proportional fraction of the asset to your portfolio.
4
Your Investment Appears in Your Portfolio
The invested amount is deducted from your available cash balance and the asset position is added to your portfolio immediately. You can track its performance in real time from the Portfolio section of your dashboard.
5
Dividends and Returns
Real estate investments pay monthly dividends automatically credited to your cash balance on the 15th of each month. Stock dividends are paid quarterly. You can reinvest dividends or withdraw them at any time.

Physical Gold Shop

The Physical Gold Shop is a separate service from regular investing. You can purchase physical gold bars, silver bars, or platinum bars and choose to have them stored in our insured vaults or shipped directly to your address.

Important: Separate Payment System The Physical Gold Shop operates independently from your Qubix cash balance. You cannot use your available cash balance to pay for physical gold orders. Each purchase requires a separate crypto payment made at the time of order. This is by design to maintain clear separation between investment accounts and physical commodity purchases.
1
Navigate to the Physical Gold Shop
Access the Vault section from the main navigation and click on Physical Gold Shop. You will see a catalog of available gold bars, silver bars, and platinum bars in various sizes (1oz, 10oz, 1kg, and larger).
2
Select Your Product and Choose a Storage or Delivery Option
Select the metal type and size you want to purchase. You must then choose one of two options:

Option A — Vault Storage: Your physical metal is stored in our Zurich vault in your name. You receive full documentation including a vault certificate and proof of ownership. First year of storage is free, then 0.5% per year.

Option B — Physical Delivery: Your metal bar is shipped to your address via insured courier within 5–10 business days. Shipping fee of $25–$50 applies based on weight and destination.
3
Complete Payment — Separate from Your Cash Balance
After selecting your product and delivery option, you will be taken to a separate payment page. The system will generate a unique wallet address and exact amount to send in your chosen cryptocurrency. This payment is completely separate from your Qubix investment account.

How to Withdraw Funds

You can withdraw your available cash balance at any time. Withdrawals are sent in cryptocurrency to a wallet address you provide. The process is straightforward and our team processes all withdrawal requests within 30 minutes to 1 hour.

1
Click "Withdraw" on Your Dashboard
Log into your account and click the Withdraw button on your main dashboard. You can only withdraw funds that are in your Available Cash Balance. You cannot withdraw funds that are currently invested in assets — you must sell the asset position first to return it to your cash balance.
2
Select Your Cryptocurrency and Enter the Withdrawal Amount
Choose which cryptocurrency you want to receive (e.g. Bitcoin, USDT, Ethereum). Enter the amount in USD you wish to withdraw. The system will display the equivalent amount in your chosen cryptocurrency at the current market rate.
3
Enter Your Wallet Address
Paste the wallet address from your external cryptocurrency wallet or exchange where you want to receive the funds. Double-check the address carefully before submitting. Always paste — do not type manually.
Verify Your Wallet Address Before Submitting Once a withdrawal is submitted and processed, it cannot be reversed. Funds sent to an incorrect address are permanently lost. Always confirm your wallet address is correct and that it matches the selected cryptocurrency network (e.g. do not send USDT TRC-20 to an ERC-20 address).
4
Submit Your Withdrawal Request
Review the withdrawal summary — amount, currency, and destination address — then click Submit Withdrawal Request. The funds will be deducted from your available cash balance immediately and your request will be queued for processing.
5
Our Team Verifies and Processes the Request
Our compliance and operations team reviews your withdrawal request to verify account security and regulatory compliance. This typically takes 30 minutes to 1 hour. You will receive an email notification once the withdrawal has been sent to the blockchain.
Processing Withdrawal
Your request is being reviewed by our team. Estimated processing time: 30–60 minutes.
6
Cryptocurrency Arrives in Your Wallet
Once processed, the cryptocurrency will be sent to the wallet address you provided. You will receive an email with the transaction ID (TXID) so you can verify the transfer on the blockchain explorer. Blockchain confirmation takes an additional 10–30 minutes depending on network conditions.
